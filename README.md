## Overview

This project presents the **AdventureWorks Insights Dashboard**, developed using Power BI to provide actionable insights into sales, customer behavior, and product performance. The dashboard enables executives and analysts to monitor key metrics, identify trends, and make informed business decisions.

This project was developed under the **guidance of Haneesha Thasni N**, whose expertise and direction helped shape the structure, design, and analytical depth of the dashboard. The guidance ensured that the project focuses on meaningful KPIs, intuitive visuals, and interactive capabilities to provide a comprehensive analytical tool for business decision-making.

---

## Dashboard Overview
The dashboard is structured to provide insights at multiple levels:

### 1. Executive Dashboard
- **Key Metrics:** Total Revenue (24.91M), Total Profit (10.46M), Total Orders (25K), Total Returns (1809), Return Rate (0.02)  
- **Revenue Trend:** Monthly revenue trends with future projections  
- **Top Categories & Products:** Accessories, Bikes, Clothing; top 10 products ranked by orders, revenue, and return rate  
- **Insights:** Most ordered product: `Water Bottle – 30 oz.`, most returned: `Road-650 Red, 52`  
- **Monthly Comparisons:** Revenue, orders, and returns compared month-over-month  

### 2. Regional Analysis
- **Interactive Map:** Orders by country with regional highlights (North America, Europe, Pacific)  
- **Highlights:** USA and Canada dominate North America; UK, France, Germany for Europe; Australia for Pacific  

### 3. Customer Analysis
- **Revenue per Customer:** 1.38K  
- **Unique Customers:** 18K  
- **Orders by Income & Occupation:** Visualized with donut charts  
- **Top 100 Customers:** Table displaying total orders & revenue  
- **Highlight:** Top Customer – `MR. Maurice Shan` with 6 orders generating 12.41K revenue  
- **Trend Analysis:** Total customers and revenue over time  

### 4. Product Analysis
- **Performance vs Target:** Monthly order, revenue, profit compared to targets using gauges  
- **Profit Trend:** Total vs adjusted profit by month  
- **Interactive Matrix:** Product-level Orders, Profit, Returns, Revenue, and Return %  
- **Product Trends:** Line charts tracking product performance over time

## Key Features
- Interactive dashboards with filters (region, product, year)  
- Executive, regional, customer, and product-level insights  
- KPIs and visual hierarchy for quick decision-making  
- Dark-themed UI for enhanced visualization  
- Revenue and profit forecasting  

---

## Data Sources
The dashboard uses the following data sources:  
- **Calendar Lookup** – for dates and time intelligence  
- **Customer Lookup** – customer demographic and contact info  
- **Product Categories Lookup** – product category hierarchy  
- **Product Lookup** – product master data  
- **Product Subcategories Lookup** – subcategory hierarchy for products  
- **Returns Data** – returns and return reasons  
- **Sales Data 2020, 2021, 2022** – historical sales transactions  
- **Territory Lookup** – mapping of sales regions and territories  

---

## Usage
- Use slicers/filters to explore data by region, year, or product  
- Hover over charts for detailed insights  
- Drill-through functionality to analyze individual product or customer details  
- Export reports or visuals for presentations and business reviews
